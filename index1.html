Minas Cocktail Room |

Collective Group

Minas is the first bar in Yerevan with an artistic twist. Stop by and grab a
drink while you get lost in the striking colors of Minas Avetisyan’s artwork.

Unlike the other bars in town, Minas is not your average cocktail room - it’s a tribute to Minas Avetisyan, the talented 20th-century Armenian painter (hence the name). And unlike other bars, Minas is partially open-air to let that fresh breeze blow in and mingle with the rest of the atmosphere. Not everyone is a fan of the poorly lit, overcrowded, smoke-filled pub or bar, which is precisely how the idea for the cocktail room was born. Minas is the place to be for those who prefer a more luxurious and intimate environment, especially if you are seeking a more aesthetic place to relax.

Yerevan Nights Just Got Better

Add a splash of inspiration to your Friday nights and important celebrations, and grab something to
drink at our colorful cocktail room. Here you’ll find the artist’s gorgeous paintings covering the bar’s
walls, combined with an intimate environment and delicious drinks to provide the ultimate sensory
experience. Order your favorite beverage, sit back, and lose yourself in the colors. And if you are feeling
adventurous, let our skilled bartenders offer you something new. Breathe in the
evening air, take in the flavors and let your gaze fall upon the beautiful paintings of Minas Avetisyan.

Opening hours: 8:00 AM - 2:00 AM
