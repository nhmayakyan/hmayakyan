Afrolab is the first specialty coffee roastery in Yerevan. Get acquainted with the place and what we have to offer to the coffee lovers of Armenia.

Afrolab is a staple part of Collective Group and the first specialty coffee roastery in Yerevan. Situated on Pushkin Street, right in the middle of the city center, Afrolab is the ideal place for coffee connoisseurs, audiophiles, interior design enthusiasts, and anyone in between. Our tropical-inspired coffee shop is the perfect place to sit down and gather your thoughts during (or after) a busy day, relax, recharge and order your favorite blend.

Specialty Coffee Meets Top-tier Service

Our latest coffee-making equipment and best baristas will be at your service to offer you timeless favorites or tempt your taste buds with some exciting in-house blends. Afrolab prides itself in meticulously picking out only the finest coffee beans and using the best equipment to create unmatched coffee blends that will have you coming back for more. Cozy up indoors, lounge back on our outdoor terrace, or grab a takeaway to drink on the go - your Afrolab experience is up to you!
