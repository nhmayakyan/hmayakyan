Gallia is a fine-dining restaurant serving your favorite European dishes in the heart of
Yerevan. Get acquainted with the idea behind the place, and check out our menu.

When historical inspiration met fine-dining, the idea for Gallia was born. This classy dining establishment was inspired by the historical region of Gaul, which is now known as modern Western Europe. The founders of Gallia decided to combine timeless dishes from the classic European cuisine with the modern concept of fine dining to create a place wher e all food preferences are catered to. Whether you are cravin g a juicy steak, a light salad, or a decadent dessert, you’ll be sure to find what you’re looking for on the menu!

Tempt Your Tastebuds

Gallia offers beloved European dishes sprinkled with a touch of gourmet dining and a pinch of creativity, while the curated design will make you feel like you’ve been here before. At Gallia, you can find the classic staples of European cuisine, as well as healthy, high-quality versions of your favorite comfort food dishes. Our diverse menu and talented chefs guarantee that you will find the dish to suit your personal preferences, mood, and cravings for the day. Whether you want to enjoy an evening alone, gather with loved ones, or stop by for a romantic date, make sure to come on an empty stomach.
